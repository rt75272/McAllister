About the Learning Center

Fun, Engaging, and Educational Games for Students

Welcome! This site is a thoughtfully curated collection of interactive math and English Language Arts (ELA) games. Our mission is to make learning an exciting and visually engaging experience for every student.

Our Educational Goals

  • To provide enjoyable, bite-sized activities that reinforce core academic skills.
  • To offer progressively challenging levels, allowing learners to grow at their own pace.
  • To deliver instant, constructive feedback that encourages practice and mastery.

How to Get Started

Simply choose a game from the home page to begin your learning adventure. Each game includes simple, clear instructions to get you started right away.

Built with: Python, Flask, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript.

This project is open-source. View the code and contribute on GitHub.
