Welcome! This site is a small collection of interactive math and ELA games designed to make math and ELA practice engaging and visual for students.

Goals

  • Provide bite sized, game like activities to practice core math and ELA skills.
  • Offer progressive difficulty where relevant so learners can improve over time.
  • Give immediate feedback and encourage repeated practice.

How to Use

Choose a game from the home page. Most games will provide simple instructions in the top area.

Built with: Python, Flask, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript.
